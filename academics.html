Academics

Academic Excellence

Our school focuses on providing strong academic foundations along with discipline, creativity and practical learning.

We encourage students to actively participate in classroom learning, activities and skill development programs.

Dedicated teachers continuously guide students to improve their confidence, knowledge and overall personality.

English

Developing communication, reading, and writing skills.

Hindi

Building strong language and grammar foundations.

Mathematics

Improving logical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Science

Exploring scientific concepts through practical learning.

Social Studies

Understanding history, geography, and society.

Improving concentration, memory, and fast calculation skills through mental math techniques.

Art & Craft

Encouraging creativity, imagination, and artistic skills among students.
