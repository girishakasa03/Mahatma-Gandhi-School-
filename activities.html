Student Activities

Co-Curricular Activities

Parents Teacher meeting

Parent-Teacher Meetings are regularly conducted to discuss students’ academic performance, discipline, and overall development. These meetings help build strong communication between parents and teachers for the better growth and success of students.

Sports Activities

Sports activities in our school encourage physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and confidence among students. Various indoor and outdoor games, athletic events, and competitions help students develop a healthy and active lifestyle.

School Events

School events provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, creativity, leadership, and confidence. Various competitions, celebrations, and special programs are organized throughout the year to encourage active participation and overall personality development.
