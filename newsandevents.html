News & Events

Latest Updates From Our School

Stay informed about celebrations, competitions, academic activities and school events.

Independence Day

Independence Day is celebrated in our school with great patriotism and pride. The celebration includes flag hoisting, patriotic songs, speeches, dances, and cultural performances by students. It helps students remember the sacrifices of our freedom fighters and inspires love for the nation.

Festivals

Our school celebrates various festivals with great excitement and happiness. Students actively participate in cultural activities, performances, and traditional events that promote unity, respect, and cultural diversity.

Annual Sports Meet

Sports Day is celebrated in our school with great energy and team spirit. Students participate in various indoor and outdoor games, athletic events, and competitions with enthusiasm. The event encourages physical fitness, discipline, confidence, and sportsmanship among students.
