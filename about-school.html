About Our School

Building Bright Futures

Established in 2014 under the guidance of Dakshin Bharat Hindi Prachar Sabha, Mahatma Gandhi E.M High School was inaugurated by Pro-Vice-Chancellor Shri R.F. Neeralkatti. Located in the heart of the city, the school has been dedicated to educating hundreds of students with quality learning and strong values.

Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's vision that education can transform the world, the institution focuses on providing affordable education to all sections of society. With experienced faculty and good facilities, the school especially supports students from underprivileged backgrounds.

The school encourages academic excellence along with participation in cultural, sports and social activities. Our teachers guide every student with care and dedication.

We continuously strive to improve our campus and facilities to provide better opportunities for students and help them grow into responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge, discipline and confidence to become responsible future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide affordable and quality education while encouraging moral values, creativity and holistic development.
