School Facilities

Mahatma Gandhi English Medium High School provides quality infrastructure and student-centered facilities that support academic excellence, discipline, creativity, and overall personality development. The school offers a positive and safe learning environment with modern classrooms, laboratories, digital learning facilities, sports activities, and co-curricular programs that help students grow academically, socially, and morally.

Science Laboratory

Our Science Laboratory provides students with practical learning experiences that improve observation, experimentation and scientific thinking.

Computer Laboratory

The computer lab is equipped with modern systems that help students develop digital and technical skills.

Playground & Sports

Our spacious playground encourages sportsmanship, teamwork and physical fitness among students.

Smart TV

Smart TVs in classrooms make learning more interactive and engaging for students. They help teachers explain concepts clearly through videos, presentations, animations, and digital learning content.

Projector

Projector-based learning helps teachers present lessons in a clear, visual, and interactive manner. It improves students understanding, attention, and classroom participation through presentations, videos, and digital content.
